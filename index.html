Yanai Elazar

I am a third year PhD student in the Natural Language Processing Lab at Bar-Ilan University, under the supervision of Prof. Yoav Goldberg. In addition, I work as a research intern at AI2 Israel.
My interests lie between Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. Specifically, I'm interested in interpretability and analysis of NLP models, biases of models and datasets, and commonsense reasoning.

Since August 2020, I am co-organizing an online NLP seminar: NLP with Friends, along with Abhilasha Ravichander, Liz Salesky and Zeerak Waseem. Join us!

I'm extremely greatful (and lucky!) to be a Google PhD Fellow.
