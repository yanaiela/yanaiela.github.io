Yanai Elazar is an Assistant Professor at Bar-Ilan University. Before that, he was a Postdoctoral Researcher at AI2 and the University of Washington, and completed his PhD in Computer Science at Bar-Ilan University. He is interested in the science of generative models, for which he develops tools and algorithms for understanding what makes models work, how, and why, with special interest in the data on which these models are trained.
